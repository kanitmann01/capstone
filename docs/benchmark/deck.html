What We Built

URL → Snapshot → 5 Detectors → Composite Score → Explanation

  • Heuristics — IP, length, suspicious chars, keyword masking
  • Content — login forms, suspicious phrases, hidden elements
  • SSL / Domain Age — cert validity, WHOIS freshness
  • Threat Intel — OpenPhish, PhishTank, VT feeds
  • Brand Recognition — FAISS + Bloom + Levenshtein for typosquats

Every detector returns evidence, not just a number.

Dataset & Training

Phase 0: grew brand inventory from 10 to 110 (top 100 banks added).

  • 1,100 legitimate — Tranco top list, filtered to login-bearing domains
  • 1,100 phishing — historical positives, deduped by host
  • 2,200 total — stratified 70/15/15 split
  • Host-disjoint — same domain never appears in train and test
